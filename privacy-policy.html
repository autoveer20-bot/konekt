Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026  ·  Effective from: April 19, 2026  ·  Company: PilotCloud (Konekt)

This Privacy Policy explains how Konekt by PilotCloud collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our WhatsApp Business Platform services. By using Konekt, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

Konekt is a WhatsApp Business Platform product developed and operated by PilotCloud, an IT services company based in India. We provide two core services:

  • WhatsApp AI Employee — an automated AI chatbot for businesses
  • WhatsApp Bulk Messages — a bulk messaging platform for businesses

For any privacy-related concerns, you may contact us at the details provided at the end of this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, and phone number during signup or consultation
  • Business name, type, and details during onboarding
  • WhatsApp Business number and business information
  • Payment information (processed securely via Razorpay — we do not store card details)
  • Content you provide for AI training (FAQs, pricing, services)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, session duration
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Log data — access times, error logs, API requests

2.3 WhatsApp Conversation Data

  • Messages sent and received through your WhatsApp Business number
  • Contact phone numbers of your customers (end-users)
  • Message timestamps and delivery status

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve our services
  • To set up and train your WhatsApp AI chatbot
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send service-related communications (billing, updates, alerts)
  • To provide customer support
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To analyze platform performance and improve user experience

We do not use your data for advertising purposes or sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • WhatsApp / Meta: To send and receive messages via the official WhatsApp Business API
  • OpenAI: Message content may be processed by OpenAI's API to generate AI responses (subject to OpenAI's privacy policy)
  • Razorpay: For secure payment processing
  • Supabase: For secure data storage and management
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Database access is restricted and authenticated
  • Payment data is handled by PCI-compliant payment processors
  • Regular security reviews and access audits

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days after cancellation
  • WhatsApp conversation logs are retained for 12 months
  • Payment records are retained as required by Indian accounting laws (7 years)
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Our platform uses essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect platform functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

Konekt is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

10. WhatsApp & Meta Compliance

Konekt operates using the official WhatsApp Business API provided by Meta. By using our platform, you also agree to comply with:

  • WhatsApp Business Policy
  • Meta's Terms of Service
  • WhatsApp's Acceptable Use Policy

You are responsible for ensuring that your use of our platform and WhatsApp messaging complies with applicable laws and WhatsApp policies, including obtaining proper consent from your customers before messaging them.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on our platform. Continued use of Konekt after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
